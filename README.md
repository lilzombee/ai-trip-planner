# AI Trip Planner

## Description
The AI Trip Planner is an innovative project designed to simplify and enhance the travel planning process. It utilizes advanced algorithms and user preferences to deliver a personalized travel itinerary.

## Features

- **Mood-Based Travel Planning**: Tailor your travel plan according to your mood, ensuring that your trip aligns with what you want to experience.
- **Budget Optimizer**: Automatically adjusts the itinerary to accommodate your budget while maximizing your travel experience.
- **Group Conflict Resolver**: Efficiently manages conflicts among group members’ preferences to create a harmonious travel plan.
- **Plan B Generator**: Provides alternatives in case of unforeseen changes, ensuring that your travel experience is smooth and enjoyable.

## Conclusion
With the AI Trip Planner, every traveler can enjoy a customized journey that meets their unique needs and desires.
